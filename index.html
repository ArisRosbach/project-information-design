Why Mozambique?

In 2011 and 2012, gas and oil multinationals ENI from Italy and Anadarko from the U.S.A. made a major discovery: a gas field containing 7000 billion cubic meters of gas, just off the coast of Cabo Delgado, Mozambique’s northernmost province. The world’s fourth largest offshore gas project. The discovery was worth hundreds of billions of dollars.
